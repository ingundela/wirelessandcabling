About Us

Wireless & Cabling provides a complete range of cabling services including office cabling, office moves and desk cable management, fibre optics, CCTV and access control.

employees are cabling experts, with many years of experience and they are BICSI/RCDD qualified and comply with BS EN ISO 9001:2000, BS EN ISO 14001:1996 and OHSAS 18001:2009, to ensure quality and reliability of network design, installation practice and training.

All of our employees satisfy three rigorous criteria. They have the right professional qualifications, top level expertise and skill and a customer focused attitude.

Unusually for our industry, the team contains a high proportion of permanent staff, many of whom have been with us since we started. In addition to their technical and team working skills, they have been trained to the highest standards of health and safety, and will always provide advice and go the extra mile for our customers
